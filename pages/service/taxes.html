Business tax and other miscellaneous taxes

OFFICE OF THE CITY TREASURER

Collection of Business tax and other Miscellaneous Taxes

Client Groups: Business establishment owners

Requirements:

  • Sworn Declaration
  • Community Tax
  • Barangay Business Clearance
  • Sanitary Permit
  • SSS Clearance
  • BIR Clearance
  • DTI Registration (SEC for Corporations, CDA registration for Cooperatives)
  • BFP Fire Clearance
  • Electrical Clearance
  • CENRO Clearance
  • Tax Clearance (Realty Tax and other taxes)

Service Schedules:Mondays to Fridays

Total Processing Time: 20 minutes

Collection of Business tax and other Miscellaneous Taxes

Total Fees/Charges:

For business taxes:

  • New business computed based on the type of Business and capital
  • Existing business computed based on the annual gross receipts
  • Mayor’s Permit – based on the kind of business
  • Sanitary Fee – uniform rate of P 200.00
  • Fire Inspection Fee – 10% of regulatory fees
  • Physical exam (health card) – P 20.00/employee
  • Exercise of Calling – P 30.00/employee
  • Tax Clearance – P 100.00
  • Business Plate – P 300.00
  • Business Sticker – P 30.00
  • Garbage Fee – P 250.00

For late payments: Additional 25% surcharge
