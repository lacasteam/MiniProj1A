Social Welfare

Office of the City Social Welfare & Development Officer

Client Groups: Indigent clients/patients

Requirements:

  • Barangay Certification of Residency
  • Certification from City Assessor’s Office that the party concerned does not own real property

Service Schedules: Mondays to Fridays

Total Processing Time: 40 minutes

Total Fees/Charges: None
