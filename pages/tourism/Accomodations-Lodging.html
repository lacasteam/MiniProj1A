Staying in Sorsogon

It’s not hard to find comfortable lodgings in Sorsogon City. The City offers hotels and pensions with excellent facility. There are also smaller, more homely accommodations for bed and breakfast, or even those who prefer home-stay accommodations. Restaurants, cafes, bars, fast food joints, little eateries, offering both local and continental cuisine, especially seafood and traditional spicy Bikolano fare, are available within the City and in most towns.

Siama Hotel

Sitio San Lorenzo Bibincahan, Sorsogon, Luzon 4700 Philippines

Siama Hotel, a romantic hotel that brings the best of Sorsogon to your doorstep.

Guest rooms offer amenities such as air conditioning, a desk, and blackout curtains, and guests can go online with free internet access offered by the hotel.

Siama Hotel features a coffee shop and baggage storage, to help make your stay more enjoyable. The property also boasts a pool and free breakfast. If you are driving to Siama Hotel, free parking is available.

If you’re looking for an Italian restaurant, consider a visit to Mango Grill, Una Pizzeria, or Cafe Isabelle, which are all conveniently located a short distance from Siama Hotel.

We’re sure you’ll enjoy your stay at Siama Hotel as you experience everything Sorsogon has to offer.

Villa Isabel

Barangay Burabod Flores Street, Sorsogon, Luzon 4700 Philippines

Welcome to Villa Isabel, your Sorsogon “home away from home.” Villa Isabel aims to make your visit as relaxing and enjoyable as possible, which is why so many guests continue to come back year after year.

Villa Isabel Hotel is a hotel offering air conditioning in the rooms.

The hotel features a 24 hour front desk, room service, and a gift shop. Plus, guests can enjoy a pool and breakfast, which have made this a popular choice among travelers visiting Sorsogon. For guests with a vehicle, free parking is available.

Villa Isabel puts the best of Sorsogon at your fingertips, making your stay both relaxing and enjoyable.
