Campaign against dengue intensifies

The City Government of Sorsogon intensifies its campaign against Dengue to prevent further outbreaks. Information education campaigns (IEC) thru radio announcements and Dengue Forums in every barangay to increase awareness are being done by the City Government of Sorsogon thru the City Health Office. A city wide massive clean-up drive is also initiated by the Liga ng mga Barangay. Identified hotspots in the Barangays of Bibincahan and Caricaran are receiving bouts of mist sprays to eliminate mosquito larvae and entomological tests are being done to quantify the abundance of the dengue causing mosquito.

The Rapid Dengue Test (RDT) of patients admitted in various hospitals within the city shows 491 confirmed cases of Dengue in Sorsogon City from January 2019 to July 6, 2019 escalating on the months of May and June. The highest cases recorded were in Bibincahan (East), Caricaran (Bacon), San Juan Roro (East), Poblacion (Bacon), Balogo and Balete (Bacon).
